6th & 7th October 2026 · Reach Conference Centre, Derby

Nuclear Supply Chain Summit 2026

Summit

📅 Save the dates – 6th and 7th October 2026! We're delighted to announce our second Nuclear Supply Chain Summit, taking place at Reach Conference Centre, Derby. The event is designed for everyone involved in the nuclear supply chain.

With major opportunities emerging across new nuclear build, decommissioning, defence, fusion and space, there's never been a better time to 'get ready to deliver...'

What can you expect?

  • The latest updates from across nuclear (market intelligence and industry insight)
  • Opportunities to connect with key players and win business
  • Discuss sector supply chain initiatives and accreditations and how they improve market access
  • Hear about the NucCol 'Sector Demand Model' and our 3,500 company database
  • Practical advice on positioning your company for future growth and success

Last year we held an impromptu evening the night before the summit — this time we are planning something a little more organised on the evening of 6th October, with the full day's conference on the 7th.

More information on registration will be coming soon. In the meantime, save the date and get in touch if you'd like to discuss sponsorship, exhibition opportunities, speaking, or attending. We look forward to welcoming our sector friends and colleagues to Derby!
