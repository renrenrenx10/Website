F4N Community

The F4N Network

At the heart of NucCol's work is a growing community of UK manufacturers who have completed or are actively participating in the Fit For Nuclear (F4N) programme. This community represents hundreds of businesses who have invested in raising their standards to meet the demands of the nuclear sector.

These companies span precision manufacturing, fabrication, specialist machining, coatings, process engineering, and more — creating a diverse, capable, and vetted pool of UK supply chain talent.

NucCol works actively to connect these companies with nuclear primes, programme teams, and government stakeholders — promoting their capabilities and helping them access the opportunities they've worked hard to earn.
