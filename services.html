Core Services

What We Deliver

Five integrated service areas — each capable of standing alone or combining to address complex supply chain challenges.

01

Supply Chain Development

NucCol is a dynamic supply chain solutions provider, specialising in enhancing the UK's manufacturing capabilities across low-carbon sectors including nuclear, hydrogen, and carbon capture. We work closely with businesses to ensure they meet sector-specific requirements and capitalise on growth opportunities.

Supply Chain Readiness Level (SCRL)

Our SCRL assessments provide an in-depth evaluation of UK manufacturers' ability to meet the demands of energy infrastructure projects — identifying gaps, assessing readiness, and offering actionable insights to maximise UK content.

Demand Modelling

We provide expert demand modelling services, forecasting future demand for key commodities — components, systems, and subsystems — offering critical insights into market trends, technological needs, and supplier capabilities.

Fit For Nuclear (F4N)

NucCol delivers the Fit For Nuclear programme — the industry standard for assessing and developing UK manufacturers' readiness to supply into the nuclear sector. Through structured assessment, gap analysis, and improvement support, F4N helps companies achieve the quality, capability, and compliance standards required by nuclear primes.
