Market Overview

Headline Numbers

Key market statistics across UK and European nuclear energy, SMR programmes, and decommissioning — providing strategic context for supply chain positioning.

UK Nuclear Investment Context

  • Hinkley Point C and Sizewell C: combined ~£50–65bn capital programme
  • UK Government SMR Programme delivered by Rolls-Royce SMR — target deployment from early 2030s
  • STEP Fusion prototype power plant at West Burton — construction estimated 2032
  • AUKUS Submarine programme: long-term cross-government commitment
  • Nuclear decommissioning across NDA estate and AGR sites: £3–4bn annual spend
  • Collectively these programmes represent tens of billions of investment and are expected to support a 29% increase in the UK nuclear workforce by 2030
  • The Midlands workforce alone is expected to increase 102% by 2030
